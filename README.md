Computers and technology have always been driving factors for me. Ever since I was a kid, I was hooked the second I had the opportunity to interact with any. Creating a new folder? Yeah, pretty much magic to me back then. This passion went along with me throughout the years and helped shape what I am right now; A Software Engineer, and I really enjoy what I do. Programming is and always will be second nature to me; whenever I face problems, I dive deep and come up with insights not only enough to solve them; but also to expand my horizons and come up with new perspectives at the same time. I have worked with many technologies, covering backend, frontend, databases, DevOps, technical SEO, etc. It never stops at that; I'm always multiple steps ahead by keeping up to date with the latest trends in software engineering and the open-source. I have contributed to a couple of libraries that get thousands of weekly downloads and built a few of my own, slowly growing in popularity and downloads, currently at 80K. I'm just getting started, and there'll be more to come soon.
